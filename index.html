Science homework - Physics pg - 4293 Question: What type of energy is stored in a stretched rubber band? Answer: Elastic potential energy Question: A moving car is travelling down the road. What type of energy does it have because it is moving? Answer: Kinetic energy Question: What energy transformation happens when a light bulb is turned on? Answer: Electrical energy is transformed into light energy (and some heat energy) Question: Which type of energy is stored in food that our bodies use? Answer: Chemical energy Question: A book is sitting on a high shelf. What type of energy does it have because of its position? Answer: Gravitational potential energy
